About Me

Currently based in the San Francisco Bay Area as an undergraduate student studying UX/UI design. I have a passion for visual design and a deep appreciation for artwork from traditional to digital. Making the shift towards UX/UI design began after I recognized my strong empathy towards people and how I always incorporated other’s feelings into my own designs rather than just my own. As an aspiring designer, I’m always eager to improve and learn from new experiences.

My Skills

User Research

Learning about who your users is an important step in the UX/UI process! My goal is to identify users' needs and pain points through interviews and user personas. Part of my process is understanding how competitors meet their user's needs and how I can implement or iterate on their model.

User Empathy

Before I'm a designer, I am just like any user who wants the product they're using to benefit them. When designing an app or website, I'm always mindful of how the users would feel while interacting with my design by applying what I learn from my user research.

UX/UI

Combining the elements of both UX and UI design provides an invaluable foundation to a product. I aim to design a UI that is not only usable, accessible and useful to a user, but also visually attractive as well. With my studies in graphic design and art, being visually creative is a talent that I constantly seek to utilize in my work.

Wireframing

I create wireframes with Figma and am knowledgable with Adobe applications like Photoshop and Illustrator. I like to enhance my work with original artwork/vectors that I make with Procreate.
